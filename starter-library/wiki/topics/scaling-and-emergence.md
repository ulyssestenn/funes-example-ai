# Scaling and emergence
What happens to language models as model size, data, and compute grow. This topic covers the smooth, predictable scaling of loss; how to allocate a compute budget optimally; the capabilities that appear discontinuously with scale; and the in-context learning that scale unlocks.

## Concepts
- [Scaling laws](../concepts/scaling-laws.md) — power-law relationships between loss and scale (Kaplan et al.).
- [Compute-optimal training](../concepts/compute-optimal-training.md) — Chinchilla's equal-scaling rule correcting Kaplan.
- [Emergent abilities](../concepts/emergent-abilities.md) — capabilities absent below a critical scale.
- [In-context learning](../concepts/in-context-learning.md) — zero/one/few-shot task adaptation without weight updates (GPT-3).
